About Us

Klinik Ibnu Sina

Klinik Ibnu Sina is a trusted general practice clinic located in Cyberjaya, Selangor, dedicated to providing accessible and quality healthcare to the local community and beyond. Our team of attentive doctors and healthcare professionals strive to deliver thorough consultations, thoughtful treatment plans, and genuine care for every patient who walks through our doors.

Whether you are visiting for a routine check-up, managing a chronic condition, or seeking treatment for an acute illness, Klinik Ibnu Sina is here for you. We are open every day of the week with extended hours, ensuring that quality medical attention is never out of reach. With reasonable pricing and a patient-first approach, we are committed to keeping you and your family healthy.

  • Open 7 days a week from 8:00 AM to midnight for extended healthcare access
  • Friendly, attentive doctors known for thorough and compassionate consultations
  • Affordable pricing for both panel and non-panel patients in Cyberjaya

What We Offer

Our Services

Professional care tailored to your needs

🏥

General Consultation

Comprehensive consultations with experienced doctors for common illnesses, infections, fever, and general health concerns.

💊

Chronic Disease Management

Regular monitoring and management of chronic conditions such as diabetes, hypertension, and other long-term health issues.

🩺

IV Drip & Emergency Treatment

On-site intravenous drip therapy and prompt treatment for acute conditions such as severe dehydration and vomiting.

Preventive Health Advice

Doctor-guided counselling on lifestyle, nutrition, and preventive measures to help patients stay healthy and avoid illness.

Panel & Non-Panel Consultations
